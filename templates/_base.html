Anagram Hunt

Choose a word length, you will be given a word that matches your chosen length. You'll have 60 seconds to find all the others words that can be formed from the letters of the given word. For your word to count, you must use all letters of the given word to form your new words. Also, you may not use the word already given. You are awarded 1 point for each solved anagram. If you score 16 points you win!

Play Now!

Fundamental Math

Choose a mathmatical operation, you will be given a problem that matches your chosen operation. You'll have 30 seconds to solve as many problems as possible. Once you place your answer inside the portal the program will quickly check it. If your answer is correct you will be awarded 1 point and given a new problem. After the game, you will be given a score for the problems you solved, try to beat your own scores!
